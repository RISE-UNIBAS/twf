How to use the Transkribus Workflow Service

  • You need an admin√Æstrator to set up a project for you and create user accounts.
  • Once you have an account, you can log and work on projects you are a member of.
  • If you don't have a project, you'll need an administrator to create one for you.
  • Once you have a project, you can upload page.xml exports from Transkribus.
    • Export a document from Transkribus.
    • Export only PAGE.xml, do not add images.
    • Export the files in the format doc-id_page_page-id.xml (Example: "134234_001_34590.xml")
    • Upload the zip into your project.
  • Once you have uploaded a document, you can extract the exported information.
    • When you tagged on Transkrinus, the tags are extracted
    • Tags are automatically normalized when they are in a dictionary
    • The transcriptions are extracted.
    • File meta data is extracted
  • You then can view your documents, pages and tags.
  • You can group and authorize your tags.
