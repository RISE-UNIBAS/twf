Context Help: Import/Export Overview

The Import/Export section provides tools for importing external data into your project and exporting your project data to various formats.

Available Options

  • Export Configuration: Configure global export settings for your project.
  • Export Documents: Export documents from your project in various formats.
  • Export Collections: Export collections you've created in various formats.
  • Export Project: Export your entire project, including all documents, collections, and dictionaries.
  • Export Tags: Export tags from your project in various formats.
  • Export Dictionaries: Export dictionaries from your project.
  • Import Dictionaries: Import dictionaries from external sources.
  • Export to Zenodo: Publish your project data to the Zenodo open science repository.

Recent Exports

The main page also displays your recent exports, their status, and options to download or delete them.
