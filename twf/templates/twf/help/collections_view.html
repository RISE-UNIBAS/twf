Your Collections

This page displays all collections associated with your project. Collections organize your documents into meaningful groups for management, analysis, and processing.

Collection List

For each collection, you can see:

  • Title: The name of the collection
  • Description: An overview of the collection's purpose
  • Item Count: The number of documents in the collection
  • Created: When the collection was created and by whom

Available Actions

  • View: Open a collection to see its contents
  • Edit: Modify collection properties
  • Create: Add a new collection to your project
  • Delete: Remove a collection from your project
