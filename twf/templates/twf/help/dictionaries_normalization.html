Dictionary Normalization

Normalization is the process of standardizing dictionary entries by linking them to established authorities or creating consistent representations.

Normalization Methods

  • External Authorities: Link entries to recognized knowledge bases
  • Manual Normalization: Create standardized forms when no external match exists
  • AI-Assisted: Use language models to suggest normalizations

External Authority Services

  • GND (Integrated Authority File): For persons, organizations, events
  • Wikidata: A comprehensive knowledge base for various entity types
  • GeoNames: For geographic locations and place names

Normalization Process

  • Enter a search term related to your dictionary entry
  • Review search results from the selected authority
  • Select the best matching entry or create a manual form
  • Save the normalized form to your dictionary entry

Best Practices

  • Always try to link to established authorities when possible
  • Be consistent in your normalization approach
  • Document your decision process for difficult cases
  • Use the same normalization source for similar entries
