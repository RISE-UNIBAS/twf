Logging Out

Logging out closes your session and returns you to the login page. You can log back into your account at any time to access your projects and continue working. Thank you for using our platform!

Why Log Out?

It's good practice to log out when:

  • You've finished your work session
  • You're using a shared or public computer
  • You need to switch to a different user account
  • You'll be away from your computer for an extended period

Session Management

For security reasons, your session will automatically expire after a period of inactivity. Logging out manually ensures that your session is terminated immediately rather than waiting for the timeout.

Logging Back In

To log back in, simply visit the login page and enter your credentials. If you've forgotten your password, use the "Forgot Password" link on the login page to reset it.
