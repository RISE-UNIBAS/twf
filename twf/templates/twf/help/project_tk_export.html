Request Transkribus Export

This page allows you to request and manage an export of your data from the Transkribus platform. Transkribus is a comprehensive platform for the automated recognition, transcription, and searching of historical documents.

Export Functionality

With this interface, you can:

  1. Request Export: Initiate a new export request to the Transkribus platform for your collection data
  2. Check Status: Monitor the status of your export job on the Transkribus platform
  3. Download Export: Retrieve your completed export when it's ready

Export Process

  1. Export Request:
    • Click the "Export Requested" button to initiate a new export from Transkribus
    • The system will communicate with Transkribus to start preparing your collection data
    • Your collection ID is automatically sent with the request
    • The status of your request will be updated in the "Current status" section
  2. Status Checking:
    • Click the "Check Status of Job" button to query Transkribus about your export progress
    • The status will be updated to reflect the current state of your export
    • Export preparation may take some time depending on the size of your collection
    • You may need to check the status multiple times until the export is complete
  3. Download:
    • Once the export is ready, click the "Download Export" button
    • The exported data will be downloaded to your computer
    • The downloaded file will contain the data from your Transkribus collection

Export Content

The Transkribus export typically includes:

  • Document Transcriptions: Text transcriptions of your documents
  • PAGE XML Files: Structured data about page layouts and text regions
  • Metadata: Information about your documents and pages
  • Recognition Results: Text recognition data and confidence scores
  • Document Structure: Information about document organization

Important Notes

  • Your Transkribus collection ID is displayed on the page for reference (e.g., 299865)
  • Export processing time varies based on collection size and Transkribus server load
  • Very large collections may take significant time to export
  • The export will include the entire collection as it exists in Transkribus
  • Ensure you have sufficient storage space for the downloaded export
  • If an export fails, you may need to retry or contact Transkribus support

Troubleshooting

  • Unknown Status: If the status shows as "Unknown," it means the system hasn't yet received status information from Transkribus
  • Export Status Unknown: The system may not be able to determine the status if there's a communication issue with Transkribus
  • Failed Export: If your export fails, check your collection ID and try requesting the export again
  • Download Issues: If you encounter problems downloading your export, ensure you have a stable internet connection and sufficient disk space

After Export

After downloading your Transkribus export, you can:

  • Import the data into your TWF project
  • Process the transcription data further using TWF tools
  • Create dictionaries and tag entities based on the imported content
  • Build collections organized around your research needs
  • Apply metadata enrichment to enhance the exported content
