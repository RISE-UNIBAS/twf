Context Help

This page allows you to view and edit your user profile. You can change your name, email address, ORCID iD, and affiliation. You can also change your password.

The user information is used to identify you in the system and to provide you with the correct permissions. It also can bet attached to exported data to provide information on who curated the data.

Your password can be changed in the "Change Password" section. You will need to enter your current password and then enter your new password twice to confirm it. If you have forgotten your password, please contact your system administrator.

The shown permissions are given on a project basis. If you have been granted access to a project, you will see the permissions you have for that project. If you have not been granted but need access to a project, please contact your system administrator.
