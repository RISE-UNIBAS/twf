TWF is a tool designed for managing and enriching document-based projects. It allows users to import large sets of documents, such as those from Transkribus or PAGE.XML formats, and enhance them with additional metadata, tags, and dictionary entries. You can browse, search, and organize documents, apply metadata, and create structured collections for export.

The tool supports importing data from various formats, managing complex metadata, and grouping information into meaningful dictionaries. Once your data is organized, you can export it in customizable formats, such as JSON, for further use in other systems or analysis environments.
